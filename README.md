# Zomato Bengaluru Restaurant Data Analysis

## Project Overview

This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on the Bengaluru Zomato Restaurant Dataset. The objective is to identify patterns and trends related to restaurant ratings, pricing, cuisines, online ordering, table booking services, and restaurant locations.

Using Python and data visualization techniques, the project uncovers valuable insights about customer preferences and the restaurant ecosystem in Bengaluru.

---

## Dataset

The dataset contains information about restaurants listed on Zomato, including:

* Restaurant Name
* Location
* Rating
* Number of Votes
* Approximate Cost for Two People
* Online Order Availability
* Table Booking Availability
* Restaurant Type
* Cuisine Served

---

## Project Objectives

* Clean and preprocess the dataset.
* Analyze restaurant ratings and customer feedback.
* Study the impact of online ordering and table booking services.
* Explore pricing trends across different locations.
* Identify the most common restaurant types and cuisines.
* Generate business insights using data visualization.

## Key Insights

* Quick Bites and Casual Dining restaurants dominate the Bengaluru restaurant market.
* Online ordering services are widely adopted by restaurants.
* Table booking services are available in only a small proportion of restaurants.
* Restaurants offering table booking generally have higher average costs.
* Restaurant ratings are mostly concentrated between 3 and 4, indicating generally positive customer feedback.
* Ratings do not show a strong dependency on online ordering availability.
* Premium restaurants are concentrated in selected areas such as Indiranagar, Lavelle Road, and Malleshwaram, although affordable alternatives are available across most locations.
* North Indian cuisine is the most popular cuisine category in the dataset.

## Conclusion

The analysis reveals that Bengaluru's restaurant market is primarily driven by quick-service and casual dining establishments. While premium restaurants exist in specific locations, affordable dining options are available throughout the city. Features such as online ordering and table booking influence restaurant operations differently, while customer ratings remain generally positive across the platform.
